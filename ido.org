IDO stands for interactive do things and is a marvelous utility. It
can greatly enhance the usability various other tools.

So lets require it

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(require 'ido)
#+end_src

and enable it

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(ido-mode t)
#+end_src
